# Day 6: Fast Eigenvalues and Abstract Vector Spaces

**🚀 Day 6 of the 111 Days of Learning challenge is complete!**

Today I learned a quick trick for computing `2x2` eigenvalues using the mean and product of the matrix, and then moved into a more abstract view of linear algebra:

- ⚡ **Fast Eigenvalue Trick:** How to find `2x2` eigenvalues quickly using the trace and determinant.
- 🧮 **Mean and Product:** How the average of the diagonal entries and the product relationship help simplify the computation.
- 📦 **Abstract Vector Spaces:** How vector spaces are not limited to arrows and matrices.
- 📈 **Functions as Vectors:** How even polynomials and functions can behave like vectors in the right setting.

This made linear algebra feel much broader and more powerful. 💡
