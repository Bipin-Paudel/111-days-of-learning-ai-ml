# Day 10: Limits, Integrals, and Higher-Order Derivatives

**🚀 Day 10 of the 111 Days of Learning challenge is complete!**

Today I went deeper into calculus and connected several big ideas:

- 📉 **Limits and L'Hôpital's Rule:** How limits help resolve indeterminate forms.
- 📏 **Integrals:** How area under a curve connects to accumulation.
- 🧠 **Fundamental Theorem of Calculus:** How differentiation and integration are linked.
- 📐 **Average Slope:** How area relates to average change over an interval.
- ⚡ **Higher-Order Derivatives:** How second and higher derivatives describe acceleration and curvature.

This made calculus feel much more connected and powerful. 💡
