# Day 4: Cross Products, Duality, and Cramer's Rule

**🚀 Day 4 of the 111 Days of Learning challenge is complete!**

Today I mastered cross products and explored how they connect algebra and geometry:

- ✖️ **Cross Products in 2D:** How to think about area through the magnitude of the cross product.
- 🖐️ **Cross Products in 3D:** How the right-hand rule gives direction to the result vector.
- 🧠 **Duality:** The mind-bending idea behind cross product computations and how geometry can be interpreted from different viewpoints.
- 📏 **Cramer's Rule:** A beautiful way to solve systems of equations using determinants.

This day made linear algebra feel even more geometric and connected. 💡
