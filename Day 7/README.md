# Day 7: Derivatives and Integrals

**🚀 Day 7 of the 111 Days of Learning challenge is complete!**

- 📏 **Integrals:** How they measure the area under a curve.
- ⚡ **Derivatives:** Why "instantaneous rate of change" is really a limit idea, and how derivatives give the best constant approximation for a changing rate.

This made calculus feel much more intuitive and geometric. 💡
