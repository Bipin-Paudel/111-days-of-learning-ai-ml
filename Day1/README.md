# Day 1: Starting the AI/ML Journey with Mathematics

**🚀 Day 1 of the 111 Days of Learning challenge is officially complete!**

Starting my AI/ML journey with Step 1 of the roadmap: Mathematics.

Today I completed the first 3 chapters of the "Essence of Linear Algebra" by 3Blue1Brown and learned:

- 📐 **Vectors:** How to translate back and forth between the geometric view (arrows in space) and the numeric view (lists of numbers), and the fundamentals of vector addition and scalar multiplication.
- 🧭 **Linear Combinations & Span:** How scaling and adding basis vectors (i-hat and j-hat) can help us reach different vectors, and what it means for vectors to be linearly dependent.
- ✨ **Linear Transformations:** Matrices aren't just random grids of numbers—they are transformations of space! A 2x2 matrix perfectly describes where basis vectors land after a transformation.

Laying the foundation before jumping into algorithms! 🧠💡
